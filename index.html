Marathon Time Predictor

Marathon pacing is tricky. Traditional calculators, like the Riegel formula, work reasonably well for short and medium distances, but research has shown they often underestimate marathon times by 10 minutes or more for many runners.
In 2014, researcher Vickers collected survey data from 2,497 runners to understand which factors truly affect race performance. The results, later published in BMC Sports Science, Medicine and Rehabilitation, suggest that pacing mistakes are a common reason runners hit “the wall” in marathons.
Use this form to enter your recent race times and weekly mileage. The calculator will use Vickers’s adjusted method to provide a more accurate marathon time prediction, helping you plan your race strategy more confidently.
Enter either one race or two races. If entering two races, the first race must be the longer distance. Times should be in hh:mm:ss format.
